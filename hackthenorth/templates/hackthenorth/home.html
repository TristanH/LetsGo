LetsGo!

LetsGo! is a tool to help you and your friends discover businesses that you'd like to go to together.

We provide an interactive map that can help you and your group decide on a place to go. Whether it be for groceries, coffee, food, or booze, LetsGo! allows you to find new businesses to enjoy!

How To:

  1. Send the URL to your friends so that they can access your LetsGo! List.
  2. Browse through the businesses near you on the map
  3. You or your friends can add businesses to the list by clicking on them.
  4. Your list will be on the right, ordered by the votes each business receives.
  5. After everyone has voted, you'll have a fair and exciting destination to go to without feeeling guilty.

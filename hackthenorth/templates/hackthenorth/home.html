LetsGo!

LetsGo! is a tool to help you and your friends discover businesses that you'd like to go to!

LetsGo! is an interactive map that can help you and your group decide on a place to go. Whether it be for groceries, coffee, food, or booze, LetsGo! allows you to find new businesses to enjoy!

How To:

  1. Send the URL to your friends so that they can access your LetsGo! List.
  2. More usage instructions!
  3. You or your friends can add businesses to the list by voting on them.
  4. The list will be ordered based on the number of votes that it receives.
